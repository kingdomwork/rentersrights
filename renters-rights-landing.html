Section 21 is gone. The Renter's Rights Act goes live 1st May 2026. Get the full legal breakdown before your portfolio is exposed.

What you'll learn

Six changes.
One hour. No fluff.

01 The end of Section 21

No-fault evictions are abolished. Understand the new Section 8 grounds and how to protect your right to possession.

02 Decent Homes Standard

New mandatory minimum property conditions apply to the private rental sector for the first time. Know exactly what's required.

03 National Landlord Database

Registration is compulsory. Understand the new national database requirements and mandatory redress schemes.

04 Rent Increase Rules

New 2-month notice periods for rent increases and a ban on rental bidding. What this means for your rent review process.

05 Pets & Periodic Tenancies

Blanket pet bans are out. Fixed-terms are being replaced. How to adapt your tenancy agreements immediately.

06 Your 2026 Compliance Roadmap

Leave with a clear action plan. What to update, what to file, and what to stop doing before May 1st.
